## When Do You Need Temporary Storage?

### Storage During Probate

Dealing with a loved one's estate is emotionally draining enough without the
added pressure of having to clear an entire house immediately. Probate can take
months, and you might not be ready to make final decisions about what to keep,
what to sell, or what to donate.

Our probate storage service gives you breathing space. We'll carefully collect
items you want to preserve, store them in secure conditions, and return them
when you've had time to think clearly. There's no pressure, no rush, and no
judgement. Just safe, dignified storage for as long as you need it.

### Storage Between House Moves

You've sold your house, but your new one isn't ready yet. Or perhaps completion
dates don't line up, leaving you with a two-week gap and nowhere to put a house
full of furniture. These situations are more common than you'd think, and
they're incredibly stressful.

Rather than imposing on family or frantically searching for last-minute
solutions, let us handle it. We'll collect everything from your old property and
store it securely until your new house is ready. Then we'll deliver it all
directly to your new address. One company, one process, one less thing to worry
about.

### Renovation and Decorating Storage

Planning a kitchen renovation? Redecorating the entire downstairs? Major
building work? You'll know that keeping furniture and belongings in the house
during these projects is a recipe for dust-covered sofas, damaged items, and
builders working around obstacles.

Our renovation storage service clears the space so your contractors can work
efficiently, and protects your furniture from dust, paint splashes, and
accidental damage. When the work's finished and the dust has settled, we'll
bring everything back. Your home gets the space it needs for a proper
renovation, and your belongings stay pristine.

### Downsizing and Retirement Storage

Helping elderly parents move from a family home to something more manageable is
an emotional process. They've accumulated a lifetime of possessions, and
deciding what fits in a smaller property takes time and careful thought.

We offer a compassionate storage solution that takes the pressure off. We can
collect furniture and belongings that won't fit in the new property, store them
safely, and give your parents time to settle in and decide what they truly need.
It's not uncommon for people to realise after a few weeks that they don't
actually need half of what they stored, making it easier to part with items when
they're ready.
